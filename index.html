📋Overview

Welcome to the Roland Digital Piano Repair resource repository! This project provides comprehensive guides, documentation, and resources for repairing Roland Digital Pianos.

Whether you're facing keyboard problems, audio issues, or general maintenance needs, you'll find valuable resources here. This repository is dedicated to helping technicians, enthusiasts, and piano owners diagnose and repair common issues with Roland Digital Pianos.

🎵Supported Models

Roland RD-250s

88-key weighted keyboard stage piano with professional sound quality

Roland RD-300s

Premium 88-key stage piano with enhanced features and capabilities

📚Repository Contents

This repository includes:

  • Repair Guides - Step-by-step instructions for common repairs
  • Technical Documentation - Specifications and technical diagrams
  • Troubleshooting - Diagnostic information for various issues
  • Maintenance Tips - Best practices for keeping your piano in optimal condition
  • Resources - Links to additional information and support

⚠️Safety & Disclaimer

Important Safety Notice

These repair guides are provided for educational purposes. Please note:

  • Some repairs may require specialized tools and expertise
  • Always disconnect power before beginning any repair work
  • If you're uncomfortable with any repair, consult a professional technician
  • Improper repairs may damage your instrument or void warranties
  • The authors are not liable for any damage resulting from following these guides
